# Maintainers

The Clearance Manifest standard is maintained by its initial author.
How the standard should be governed for the long term is deliberately an open
question (maintainer-stewarded now, with managed-community, formal-standards-body,
and independent-consortium models all on the table); see the open-governance
section of [`GOVERNANCE.md`](GOVERNANCE.md) and the pinned governance issue.

## Becoming a maintainer

As community contributors emerge, governance moves toward consensus among
maintainers, with explicit promotion criteria. Sustained, high-quality
contribution (spec proposals carried through review, conformance cases, harness
or verifier ports) is the path. See [`GOVERNANCE.md`](GOVERNANCE.md) and
[`CONTRIBUTING.md`](CONTRIBUTING.md).
